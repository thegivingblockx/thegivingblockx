Thanks to a generous donation match offer from Jared Isaacman, Executive Chairman of The Giving Blocks parent company, Shift4, all donations to organizations participating in the Texas Flood Emergency Response Fundraiser from July 7th to July 31st, 2025 will be matched.

Help us reach our $1,000,000 goal to support residents, businesses, first responders, pets, and wildlife with shelter, food, medical care, and more. For more information on the matching campaign see FAQ below.

Flooding in central Texas has killed at least 95 people over the weekend, many of them children. (Photo courtesy of Fort Worth Fire Department) via Direct Relief.

Torrential rains and catastrophic flash flooding have devastated large parts of central Texas, particularly Kerr County, where more than 100 lives have been lostmany of them children attending summer camp along the Guadalupe River. This disaster has become one of the states worst in decades, with damage estimates ranging from $18 billion to $22 billion.

Entire communities have been upended, with homes, businesses, and lives swept away in a matter of hours. First responders from across the state and country are still working to recover the missing and support survivors.

Heres how to help residents affected by the devastating Texas flooding

You can donate with crypto, stock, DAF or card through The Giving Block to directly support trusted nonprofits providing emergency relief, medical aid, shelter, and long-term recovery in affected areas. Your donation will be matched for an immediate and lasting impact.

Crypto Philanthropy Support

The Giving Block set up an emergency response fundraiser to support charitable organizations working in the affected areas in Texas to assist with humanitarian and disaster relief efforts. Donations to the charities on the ground provided emergency medical care, food, shelter, and other forms of aid to those impacted.

You can still support organizations that participated in our Texas Flood Emergency Response Fundraiser, which accepts donations of crypto, stocks, DAF grants, and cards, heres what you can do to help:

  1. Select one of the organizations participating in this fundraiser below.
  2. In the organizations donation form, select a donation method and the amount you plan to give.
  3. Enter additional details, such as your name and address, and customize your donation (e.g. make it recurring). Options may vary depending on the gift type.
  4. Be sure to enter your email if you wish to receive a gift acknowledgment for tax purposes.
